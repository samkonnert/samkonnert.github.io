Sam Konnert

I'm a freelance journalist and photographer who covers the confluence of sports, culture and technology.

My day job is as a regional reporter in Northwest B.C., where I write about pipeline protests, heavy industry, Indigenous issues and junior hockey scandals.

I've worked as an associate producer and sound engineer for CBC podcasts. I have also written for The Walrus and Canadian Evergreen magazines.

Based in Ottawa, I love soccer and Seinfeld, and always clap when the plane lands.
